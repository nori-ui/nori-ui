## At a glance

- **Tones:** `neutral`, `primary`, `success`, `warning`, `danger`.
- **Appearances:** `soft` (default), `solid`, `outline`. Soft is calm and readable; solid is loud and used sparingly.
- Use sparingly. Every badge draws the eye, so a row of five stops being a row of badges.
